Ramiro Oblitas is a Creative Director with more than 20 years of international experience working extensively across a range of sectors, from fashion and beauty to entertainment, art, publishing, technology, aviation, and real estate. He places critical thinking, imagination, and craft at the heart of his practice and strives to create work that is intelligent, dynamic, and beautiful. Ramiro is a creative catalyst passionate about facilitating positive change in both people and brands from the inside and out. He is a people-centric brand-building professional with unique experience in establishing specialist talent and growing creative teams. His specialty is overseeing the design of a business's every touchpoint and solidifying a fluid visual narrative that can maximise efficiency and purpose. Ramiro is multilingual with native proficiency in Spanish, Swedish, English, and elementary proficiency in Brazilian Portuguese.
